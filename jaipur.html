NEW DELHI TO JAIPUR

Traveling from New Delhi to Jaipur is a wonderful experience for travelers. As far as the distance covered between New Delhi and Jaipur is concerned, the shortest route is 303 km, while the longest route stretches up to 314 km. And, depending on the route, the travel time can vary between 5 hours and 7 hours.There are plenty of options to get to Jaipur, but nothing beats the joy of a road trip from Delhi becauseyou get to explore beautiful places along with the rustic towns and villages along the route. The best route is:from Delhi To Jaipur Via The Neemrana.

JAIPUR ATTRACTIONS

The capital of Rajasthan, Jaipur is rich in historical legacy and often called the ‘Pink City’. It is considered India’s one of the first planned cities. With the majestic forts, historical monuments, scrumptious delicacies and colourful bazaars, it serves as a major tourist attraction in India.Some of the popular places to visit in Jaipur are: Amber Fort, Nahargarh Fort, Jal Mahal, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Chokhi Dhani.Notably, lakhs of tourists visit Jaipur during grand fairs and festivals as the city remains most vibrant and liveliest during that time. Also, it serves as a getaway to those who want to head to the desert cities of Jodhpur and Jaisalmer.

TRANSPORTATION


The average road distance between these two cities is 269km, with the average time taken to cover this distance is 05 hrs 58 mins.There are 94 buses operating between Delhi and Jaipur. Jaipur is conveniently located on National Highway No. 8, which connects to Delhi

Jaipur has an international airport which has many flights to New Delhi. Airport is located in Sanganer, which is about 13 kilometres from the centre of the city. Domestic airlines such as Air India, GoAir, IndiGo,SpiceJet etc cater to New Delhi.

There are around 28 trains running from New Delhi to Jaipur on a daily basis. Distance from New Delhi to Jaipur By Train 303 Km.DOUBLE DECKER(12985) is the fastest train and Pbr Mfp Express(19269) is the cheapest train on this route. For bookings,visit IRCTC website or you can directly head to the nearest railway reservation offices.
