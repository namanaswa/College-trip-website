NEW DELHI TO KASOL

Kasol is a backpacker’s delight as the scenic terrains and the picturesque sights all around are perfect for hiking and trekking. There is no direct transport mode connectivity between New Delhi and Kasol. The cheapest way to reach from New Delhi to Kasol is bus to Kullu, then cab to Kasol and takes 12h 50m. The fastest way to reach from New Delhi to Kasol is flight to Kullumanali Airport, then cab to Kasol and takes 3h 51m. The recommended way to reach from New Delhi to Kasol is train to Chandigarh, then cab to Kasol and takes 10h 16m.

KASOL ATTRACTIONS

Kasol is popular with all types of visitors due to the excellent opportunities it provides for adventure seekers as well as fun-seekers. You can spend your days walking by the river stream, shopping for colorful woolen clothing, taking simple strolls through the jungle, or fishing in Naggar Village for a laid-back holiday. But if you’re looking to let your inner adventurer loose, Kasol has some pretty awesome riverside campsites and also serves as the basecamp for a variety of exciting treks. Some of the exciting expeditions you can take from Kasol include the Kheerganga Trek, Sar Pass Trek, Chalal Trek, and Pin Parvati Pass Trek

TRANSPORTATION


The distance between the two destinations is about 565 km, and it takes 12 hours or so to reach Kasol. Several state transport buses ply from the ISBT-Kashmere Gate bus stand in Delhi on a daily basis. The best option is to take an overnight trip from Delhi to Bhuntar.

Air trippers can take a flight from Delhi to Bhuntar, and then opt for a bus or cab ride to Kasol. Transport services are easily available at the Bhuntar airport. The best season to visit Kasol is between March and June, and between December and February.

Train travellers can take a train from Delhi to Chandigarh. Alight from the train at Chandigarh and get aboard a HRTC bus bound for Kullu or Manali. All buses going towards Manali or Kullu make a pit stop at Bhuntar. From Bhutar, tourists need to take another bus or cab for Kasol.
