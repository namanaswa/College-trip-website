NEW DELHI TO GOA

BannerImage About Goa Goa is a favored tourist destination of people from around the world. With its vast stretches of silvery beaches caressed by the sparkling blue Arabian Sea, this city offers its visitors a medley of old-world charm and modern sophistication. The cheapest way to reach from New Delhi to Goa is train to Pune Jn, then bus to Goa and takes 38h 15m. The fastest way to reach from New Delhi to Goa is flight to Goa International Airport and takes 2h 25m. The recommended way to reach from New Delhi to Goa is flight to Goa International Airport and takes 2h 25m.

GOA ATTRACTIONS

Goa is a home to a diverse community which follows different religions such as Hinduism, Christianity, Islam, Sikhism and Buddhism. Different festivals of celebration such as Christmas, Easter, Goan Carnival, Diwali, Shigmo etc attract tourists from all over the world. The water sports in Goa attract a large number of tourists from all over the world. Some of the common water sports offered at popular beaches of Goa are jet-skiing, parasailing, banana boat rides, snorkelling and water scooter rides.Some of the popular places to visit in Goa are: Dudhsagar Waterfalls, Baga Beach, Candolim Beach, Anjuna Beach, Fort Aguada.
